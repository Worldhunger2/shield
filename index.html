What is world Hunger ?

World hunger refers to hunger aggregated to the global level. Related terms include food insecurity and malnutrition.
Food insecurity refers to limited or unreliable access to foods that are safe and nutritionally adequate (National Research Council, 2006).
Malnutrition is a condition resulting from insufficient intake of biologically necessary nutrients (National Research Council, 2006).
Although malnutrition includes both overnutrition and undernutrition, the focus for global hunger is undernutrition.
